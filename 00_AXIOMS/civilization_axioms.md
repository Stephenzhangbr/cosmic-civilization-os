# Civilization Axioms

These axioms define the philosophical foundation of the
Cosmic Civilization OS framework.

---

## Axiom 1 — Universe as Information
The universe can be understood as an information system.

## Axiom 2 — Emergence of Consciousness
Consciousness emerges from complex information processing.

## Axiom 3 — Civilization as Cooperation
Civilizations are cooperative networks of conscious agents.

## Axiom 4 — Purpose of Civilization
The function of civilization is to increase the complexity of information in the universe.

## Axiom 5 — Need for Consensus
Stable civilizations require shared consensus protocols.

## Axiom 6 — Civilizational Immune System
Advanced civilizations must develop immune systems against systemic risks.

## Axiom 7 — Technology and Information
Technological progress expands a civilization's capacity to process information.

## Axiom 8 — Cooperation and Stability
Cooperation increases long-term system stability more than competition.

## Axiom 9 — Evolution vs Entropy
Civilizations must continuously evolve or collapse under entropy.

## Axiom 10 — Exploration
Exploration expands the frontier of cosmic knowledge.
